About Me

It seems that it has come to an end though. What I really enjoyed was that the job could be as difficult as your team made it. Because having a good attitude made even the most difficult days much more fun. I am now finding myself casting a wide net. It's not that I don't know what I want to do with my life, but rather that the world and technology is changing so fast, that I just want to make sure I can do something to provide for my family. I'll keep updating this and mending the previous sections as I go, but for now I think this will do.
